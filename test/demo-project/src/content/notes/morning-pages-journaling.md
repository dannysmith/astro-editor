# Morning Pages Journaling

Trying out **morning pages** from *The Artist's Way*. The concept: write three pages longhand, first thing every morning, stream-of-consciousness style.

## The Rules

- Three pages, no more, no less
- Longhand only (no typing)
- Do it *first thing* in the morning
- Don't read what you wrote
- No editing, no censoring

## My Experience So Far

Two weeks in and I'm noticing:

- My mind feels clearer throughout the day
- I'm working through problems without realizing it
- The act of writing slows my racing thoughts
- It's meditative in a weird way

Might not be for everyone, but it's working for me so far.

### Equipment Needed

- Pour-over dripper (V60, Chemex, etc.)
- Gooseneck kettle
- Burr grinder
- Scale
- Timer

## The Technique

The article describes a **three-pour method**:

1. **Bloom** - wet grounds, wait 30-45 seconds
2. **Main pour** - slow, circular motions
3. **Final pour** - finish to desired weight

Total brew time should be 2.5-3.5 minutes.
